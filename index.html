Introduction


I am an Assistant Professor in Economics at Aalborg University. A major part of my research is devoted to developing macroeconomic models with a focus on Stock-Flow Consistent approach. My research work has a strong empirical focus. I am currently involved in several projects, which broadly includes Monetary policy and Securitisation, Inequality, Empirical Stock-Flow Consistent Macroeconomic models. My teaching portfolio is composed of several subjects, including Financial economics, Econometrics, Statistics, Macroeconomics, Economic modelling, Financial instability and crises.
